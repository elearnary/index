Copyright Removal Request

Elearnary is an educational platform providing access to books and materials for learning purposes. We acknowledge that some materials may be copyrighted by their respective publishers.

If you are a copyright owner or representative and believe any content on Elearnary infringes your copyrights, please contact us immediately:

  • Email: blearnary@gmail.com
  • Subject Line: "Copyright Removal Request"

In your request, please include:

  1. A physical or electronic signature of the copyright owner
  2. Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to be infringed
  3. Specific URL(s) where the material is located on our site
  4. Your contact information (address, telephone number, email)
  5. A statement that you have a good faith belief the use is not authorized
  6. A statement that the information in the notification is accurate

We will promptly investigate all valid claims and remove infringing content within 48 hours of verification.
